As part of the product design course, the Botanic - Garden Assistant project was created together with the team.

Description:

Our mobile application is a garden assistant, which is aimed primarily at beginners who want to take care of a particular plant, but do not know where to start, and are not privy to the process of care and harvesting. In addition, our application will be a good tool for experienced gardeners and gardeners. The main value is that it helps in the care of plants by providing various information on sowing, fertilizing process, harvesting, the list of necessary seeds, thereby saving users time to search for such information. Unlike many competitors, the application solves the problem associated with the complexity of plant care, taking into account the weather conditions, climatic and geographical features of the selected area. The application will facilitate the process of care, because it will know whether it has rained in the area of your site or not, whether it has turned cold, and will tell you all this information! In addition, our assistant will select the best plants for your region based on all this information.
